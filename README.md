### 👋 About

Microbiology by degree, builder by breaking and building things. My focus is **bioenergy and environmental biotechnology** — turning organic waste into **biogas** by understanding, and optimising, the microbes that do the work. My thesis was on *microbial electron transfer*; my sights are set on **anaerobic digestion and the microbial ecology behind it.**

I work at **biogas × computational microbiology** — bringing bioinformatics, metagenomics, and code to a wet-lab science, and shipping the odd web app on the side. Heading toward graduate research, and eventually a **biogas / biotech venture.**

### 🧭 What I'm into

- ⚡ &nbsp;**Bioenergy & biogas** — anaerobic digestion · microbial electron transfer · waste-to-energy
- 🧬 &nbsp;**Biotechnology** — microbial ecology · bioremediation · antimicrobial resistance
- 🧠 &nbsp;**Computational microbiology** — bioinformatics · metagenomics · ML on microbiome data
- 💻 &nbsp;**Building** — web apps · tools · AI-powered things
