### 👋 About

Microbiologist by training, builder by instinct. My focus is **bioenergy and environmental biotechnology** — turning organic waste into **biogas** by understanding, and optimising, the microbes that do the work. My thesis was on *microbial electron transfer*; my sights are set on **anaerobic digestion and the microbial ecology behind it.**

Computation is my edge, not my headline — I bring **bioinformatics, metagenomics, and a coding background** to a wet-lab science, and ship the odd web app on the side. Heading toward graduate research, and eventually a **biogas / biotech venture.**

### 🧭 What I'm into

- ⚡ &nbsp;**Bioenergy & biogas** — anaerobic digestion · microbial electron transfer · waste-to-energy
- 🧬 &nbsp;**Environmental biotech** — microbial ecology · bioremediation · antimicrobial resistance
- 🖥️ &nbsp;**Computation** *(the tool)* — bioinformatics · metagenomics · ML on microbiome data
- 💻 &nbsp;**Building** — web apps · tools · AI-powered things
